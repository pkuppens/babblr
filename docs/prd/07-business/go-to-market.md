# Go-to-Market Strategy

## Purpose

Distribution, marketing, and customer acquisition strategy.

## Launch Phases

### Phase 1: Open Source Community (Current)
**Target**: Developers, early adopters, privacy-conscious learners

**Channels**:
- GitHub
- Hacker News
- Reddit (r/languagelearning, r/opensource)

**Messaging**:
- "Open-source alternative to Duolingo"
- "Privacy-first AI language tutor"
- "BYOAPI - you control costs"

**Goals**: 100+ GitHub stars, 1,000 MAU

### Phase 2: Product Hunt Launch (MVP Complete)
**Target**: Self-directed learners, tech enthusiasts

**Channels**:
- Product Hunt
- Tech blogs
- Language learning communities

**Goals**: Top 10 on Product Hunt, 10,000 MAU

### Phase 3: Commercial Licensing (Post-MVP)
**Target**: EdTech companies, corporate training

**Channels**:
- Direct sales
- B2B partnerships
- Educational conferences

**Goals**: 10-20 commercial customers, $50K-100K ARR

### Phase 4: Mobile Expansion (v3.0+)
**Target**: Broader consumer market

**Channels**:
- App stores (iOS, Android)
- Social media ads
- Influencer partnerships

**Goals**: 100,000 MAU

## Marketing Messaging

### Value Propositions
1. **Natural Conversation**: Practice real conversation, not drills
2. **Privacy-First**: Your data stays local
3. **Affordable**: BYOAPI or free with Ollama
4. **Open Source**: Transparent, community-driven

### Target Segments

**B2C**: Self-directed learners (career, travel, immigration)
**B2B**: EdTech companies, language schools, corporate training
