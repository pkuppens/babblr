# Business Model

## Purpose

Revenue streams, cost structure, and financial strategy.

## Revenue Streams

### 1. Commercial Licensing (Primary)
**Target**: EdTech companies, corporate training, language schools

**Pricing**: Custom pricing based on:
- Number of users
- Feature access
- Support level
- Customization needs

**Revenue Target**: $50K-100K ARR
**Customer Target**: 10-20 commercial customers

### 2. Professional Services (Future)
- Custom development
- Integration consulting
- Training and onboarding

### 3. Sponsorships/Donations (Future)
- GitHub Sponsors
- Corporate sponsors
- Open Collective

## Cost Structure

### Development
- **Current**: Open-source volunteer contributors
- **Future**: Full-time maintainers (funded by revenue)

### Infrastructure
- **Minimal**: No cloud infrastructure (local-first)
- **CI/CD**: GitHub Actions (free for open-source)
- **Hosting**: GitHub Pages (free)

### User Costs
- **LLM APIs**: User-controlled (BYOAPI)
- **Local Models**: Free (Ollama)

## Financial Projections (3 Years)

| Year | Commercial Customers | ARR | Costs | Profit |
|------|---------------------|-----|-------|--------|
| 2026 | 2-5 | $10K-25K | $5K | $5K-20K |
| 2027 | 10-15 | $50K-75K | $25K | $25K-50K |
| 2028 | 20-30 | $100K-150K | $50K | $50K-100K |

## Business Risks

1. **Low Adoption**: Mitigate with strong community building
2. **Competition**: Differentiate on privacy, open-source, BYOAPI
3. **LLM Costs**: Offer Ollama for free local alternative
