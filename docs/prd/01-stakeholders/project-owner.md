# Project Owner Stakeholder

## Purpose

This document defines the needs and objectives of the project owner, who is responsible for intellectual property protection, business sustainability, and strategic direction.

## Project Owner Needs

### 1. Intellectual Property Protection

**Needs**:
- Maintain ownership of copyrights
- Protect "Babblr" trademark
- Ensure dual licensing is legally enforceable
- Prevent unauthorized commercial use

**How Addressed**:
- **Copyrights**: Maintained through Contributor License Agreement (CLA)
- **Trademark**: "Babblr" trademark reserved (see [TRADEMARKS.md](../../../TRADEMARKS.md))
- **Dual Licensing**: AGPL-3.0 + Commercial (see [licensing-strategy.md](../07-business/licensing-strategy.md))
- **CLA**: Required for all contributors (see [CLA.md](../../../CLA.md))

---

### 2. Sustainable Revenue Model

**Needs**:
- Generate revenue to sustain development
- Fund full-time maintainers (future)
- Cover infrastructure costs
- Ensure long-term viability

**How Addressed**:
- **Commercial Licensing**: Revenue from enterprises using Babblr in proprietary products
- **Professional Services**: Consulting, custom development (future)
- **Sponsorships**: GitHub Sponsors, corporate sponsors (future)

**Target**: $50K-100K ARR from commercial licensing

**See**: [business-model.md](../07-business/business-model.md)

---

### 3. Community Growth While Protecting Commercial Interests

**Needs**:
- Grow open-source community
- Maintain contributor relationships
- Protect commercial licensing opportunity
- Balance open-source values with business needs

**How Addressed**:
- **Transparent Licensing**: Clear communication about dual licensing
- **AGPL-3.0**: Prevents proprietary forks without contributing back
- **CLA**: Enables dual licensing while maintaining open-source release
- **Contributor-Friendly**: Responsive maintainers, clear docs, recognition

**Trade-Offs**: CLA adds friction, but necessary for dual licensing (see [trade-offs.md](../08-decisions/trade-offs.md))

---

### 4. Risk Management

**Needs**:
- Protect against legal risks
- Manage security vulnerabilities
- Handle contributor disputes
- Ensure code quality

**How Addressed**:
- **Legal**: CLA, licensing docs, POLICIES.md
- **Security**: Dependabot, CodeQL, security scanning (see [security-requirements.md](../06-technical/security-requirements.md))
- **Disputes**: Clear CONTRIBUTING.md, CODE_OF_CONDUCT.md
- **Quality**: CI/CD, code review, testing requirements
